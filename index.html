About Me

After obtaining my B.Sc. in Industrial and Production Engineering in 2020, I embarked on a freelancing journey on Upwork due to the COVID-19 pandemic lockdown that limited local job opportunities. This transition proved to be transformative, allowing me to work in diverse fields while focusing on areas directly relevant to my major that demanded problem-solving, critical thinking, creativity, and strong technical skills.

My freelancing expertise encompasses highly technical skills including computer-aided design, computer-aided engineering, simulation, finite element analysis, and design for manufacturability. These competencies not only advance my career but also support my future research endeavors in solving complex industrial challenges.

Parallel to my freelancing work, I have been actively engaged in research and academic writing. To date, I have completed five research articles: two published in Q1 and Q2 journals respectively, one recently accepted for publication, and two additional articles available as preprints. Additionally, I achieved certification as a CSWA (Certified SolidWorks Associate in Mechanical Design) with an outstanding score of 235 out of 240.
