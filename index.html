I, Md Nazmul Hasan Dipu, obtained a B.Sc. in Industrial and Production Engineering in 2020. Afterward, I commenced freelancing on Upwork because, at that time, I was unable to look for local jobs due to the lockdown, which was a consequence of the COVID-19 pandemic. Albeit freelancing provided a wide variety of working fields, I involved myself in a category that was not only pertinent to my major but also required problem-solving, critical thinking, creativity, and strong technical skills that would be beneficial for both my career and future research work. Most of those freelancing skills were highly technical — computer-aided design, computer-aided engineering, simulation, finite element analysis, and design for manufacturability — that will help me in the future, either directly or indirectly, to solve onerous issues. Another point that I should mention is that I worked on research and review papers at the same time. As a result, five articles have been carried out till now. Two of them have already been published: one in a Q1 journal and another in a Q2 journal. One more article has just been accepted. Plus, preprints have been posted for the remaining two articles. In addition to this, I can add that I took a test, which was CSWA (Certified Solidworks Associate in Mechanical Design), and achieved a score of 235 out of 240.
